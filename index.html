About Me

Hey there, I'm Julie Bartz, the creative mind behind JB Designs! By day, I'm in the classroom shaping young minds as a second-grade teacher, but when the school day ends, I dive into the world of crafting at my home in Livingston, Montana.

I use many tools such as my Cricut and sublimation to turn ordinary items into personalized treasures. From tumblers to earrings, mugs to shirts, each piece is made with care and a touch of Montana charm.

I'm a proud graduate of Montana State University in Bozeman, and my love for both crafting and education runs deep. I'm always looking for new projects and ideas, so feel free to reach out and let me know what you're looking for! Thanks for stopping by JB Designs, let's make something special together!
